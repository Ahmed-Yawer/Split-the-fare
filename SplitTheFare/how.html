Split The Fare

Is a taxi share app with focus primarily in the corporate market for example airports to business/corporate parks and city centres, but with the potential to scale to consumer market particularly commuters from residential to business parks where public transport doesn't exist or is poor. The main benefits would be providing direct to door transport at lower/affordable cost.
